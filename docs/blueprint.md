# **App Name**: Vetro Italia

## Core Features:

- Product Showcase: Display high-quality images of crystal glass mosaics, glass tiles, wash basins, counters, bowls, and bathroom furnishings.
- Parallax Scrolling: Implement a parallax scrolling effect to create a visually engaging and immersive user experience as users navigate the site.
- Product Categories: Categorize products to facilitate easy browsing.
- Product Details: Provide detailed product descriptions for each item, including dimensions, materials, and design inspiration.
- Contact Form: Allow users to contact the company with inquiries about products or custom orders.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft blue (#7B82B3) to evoke a sense of serenity and sophistication.
- Background color: Off-white (#F2F3F7) to provide a clean and modern backdrop.
- Accent color: Light violet (#A29FD7) to add subtle contrast.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for a clean, modern aesthetic.
- Full-screen, high-resolution imagery showcasing the product range, combined with parallax scrolling effects for depth and immersion.
- Simple, elegant line icons to represent product categories and other UI elements.
- Subtle animations and transitions to enhance user engagement without being distracting.
